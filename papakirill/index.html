Papa Kirill's Pizzeria

This text-based RPG was developed by a team of students. There is a bit of documentation on the Github wiki, so give it a whirl.

I specifically worked on the evidence system, the combat/dancing system and a few bits of bug fixing. In a poll of fellow students, our game won the most votes (I think due to the engaging story), and as a result I won a copy of the SQL Pocket Guide.

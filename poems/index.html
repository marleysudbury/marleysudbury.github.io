Cold Mind Morning September 2017

Distant brunette horoscopes
Yet another love story trope
Never approaching;
Scarcely approached
Taboo topics never broached
Bells in heads start to ring
Putting two together
Living happily forever
Who could have seen this coming?
Apart from I
Who sees it all sat in the sky.

Undeniable Beauty 26/2/18

I've never felt, as I've felt for thee

Though my eyes be bad, still I see
    Thou art mine choicest lover

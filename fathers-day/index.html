The pattern generated below is known as phyllotaxis, like how seeds are arranged in a sunflower. Unlike in nature, however, the angle of rotation and the colours of the dots are determined by our ages.

Let angle a be such that a = (n * (53 + 21))°, where n is the ordering number of the floret / seed / dot.

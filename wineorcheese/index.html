Choose one.

You are in the middle of a forest clearing. You have a bottle of fine vintage red wine. What do you do?

You are on a small row boat in the middle of a great lake. You can not make out any distinct shore owing to a thick fog. You have a heavy wheel of cheese. What do you do?

The cheese has a musky palette. You wish you had a bottle of fine vintage red wine to wash it down. It is incredibly filling.

The wine is incredibly wet. You feel it would go well with a musky cheese. A burning sensation quickly spreads through your body, and then disapears. What do you do?

You scramble through the thick branches and nettles for hours, mostly in pith blackness. When at last you suddenly emerge, you are at the base of a steep cliff face. What do you do?

You slowly fall into an uneasy sleep, full of night terrors, despite it being near mid-day. You are awoken by a loud growl from beyond the trees. What do you do?

There are no oars on the boat. You furiously try to paddle with your hands, but the boat never moves, and eventually they fall off due to frostbite.

You jump into the water with 'all the grace of reversing dump truck with no tires on,' but quickly discover you lack the ability to swim, or even stay afloat. As you sink into the abyss, you feel the life fade out of you.

You have died.

Above is a little gallery of images I shot with my Olympus OM-10, I believe on Fujifilm's Fujicolour C200, but it was a while ago and my memory's a little fuzzy. I think that shooting film is better…er, easier, as it naturally looks good without the need for editing that comes with digital photos.
